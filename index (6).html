While there got trained to carry out Fire watching, CSE (confined space entry) and BA (breathing apparatus). The role of Firewatching is prevention of fires by use of fire blankets and oxygen level readings being taken. CSE is when a staff member enters a confined space to carry out work and the CSE standby takes Oxygen, Pentane and Hydrogen meter readings at regular 30-minute intervals. The role of BA is when one or more members of staff carries out work that requires use of breathing apparatus and a BA standby is in charge of keeping the BA equipment full of oxygen by changing oxygen bottles every so often.
